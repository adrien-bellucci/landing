Here at Klaapi we believe that traveling should be a right and not a privilege. We believe that money shouldn't be an issue. We believe that nature, forests, rivers and birds are worth more than any 5 stars hotels you can find. Thanks to our network of hosts, we provide you with free spots to put your tent throughout Europe! You can now travel freely and not be worried to be awakened by the police in the morning because you had set up your camp in the wrong spot the night before.

The sky and the stars are your roof.
